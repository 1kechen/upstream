About UpSTREAM
  • UpSTREAM teaches STREAM (Science, Technology, Robotics, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) to Seattle students in grades 3-5, working to bridge gaps caused by disproportionate access to STREAM opportunities through accessible and culturally-relevant STREAM education.

  • UpSTREAM offers STREAM Labs, a quarterly in-person workshop, LiveSTREAMs, a monthly online workshop, and Online Tutoring, a weekly 1:1 tutoring service. These programs allows students to explore STREAM accessibly!
Behind UpSTREAM
  • UpSTREAM was founded by Seattle high school students driven by their ambition to contribute to their diverse community through STREAM education. Since its establishment, students from high schools across the city have volunteered for all three of UpSTREAM's programs.

  • We ensure that our instructors exceed rigorous standards to provide students with the best experience possible. UpSTREAM takes pride in fostering an environment where all students can safely practice curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking!
Choose UpSTREAM
  • Studies show that the earlier students explore STREAM, the more favorable they will be to the topic in their adult lives. With this timeframe in mind, UpSTREAM provides unique hands-on STREAM opportunities that are guaranteed to stir the curiosity of any student in any setting.

  • Servicing over 300 students citywide, UpSTREAM has experience with students and families from all walks of life. Regardless of a student's background in STREAM, UpSTREAM will exceed every student's expectations!
