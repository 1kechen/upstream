Supporting Students on Their Journey UpSTREAM

About UpSTREAM
  • UpSTREAM is a program teaching STREAM (Science, Technology, Robotics, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) to Seattle-area students.

  • Underprivileged students are often harmed by disproportionate access to STREAM opportunities; UpSTREAM works to bridge this gap.

  • Outside of STREAM-oriented opportunities, we offer daily virtual homework help too!

  • Learn more about STREAM by visiting the "STREAM" page!

Behind UpSTREAM
  • Certified South Seattle high school students created UpSTREAM to contribute to their diverse community.

  • Our instructors utilize all of their valuable experience to help your child move UpSTREAM.

  • We work to provide engaging, hands-on opportunities for all of our amazing students!

  • Feel free to contact our instructors by visiting the "Contact" page!

Choose UpSTREAM
  • UpSTREAM provides unique STREAM opportunities that will stir the curiosity of any student.

  • Studies show that students are more favorable to STREAM if they learn about it early on.

  • We work to meet and exceed student needs, regardless of relation to the STREAM sector.

  • To apply for UpSTREAM Programs, visit the "Apply" page!
