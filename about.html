Moving Our Future UpSTREAM

About UpSTREAM
  • UpSTREAM teaches STREAM (Science, Technology, Robotics, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) to Seattle students, working to bridge gaps caused by disproportionate access to STREAM opportunities in South Seattle.

  • UpSTREAM offers three free programs: STREAM Labs, a quarterly in-person workshop; LiveSTREAMs, a monthly online workshop; and Online Tutoring, a weekly 1:1 tutoring service. This diverse selection of programs allows students to explore STREAM frequently and flexibly!

Behind UpSTREAM
  • UpSTREAM was founded by South Seattle high school students driven by their ambition to contribute to their diverse community. Since its establishment, students from all across the city have volunteered for UpSTREAM.

  • All UpSTREAM instructors are trained to ensure they meet rigorous qualifications to provide students with the best experience possible. UpSTREAM takes pride in fostering an environment where all students can safely practice curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking!

Choose UpSTREAM
  • Many studies show that the earlier students explore STREAM, the more favorable they will be to the topic. With this in mind, UpSTREAM provides unique STREAM opportunities that are guaranteed to stir the curiosity of any student.

  • Servicing over 300 South Seattle students, UpSTREAM has experience with students and families from all walks of life. Regardless of a student's preconceptions about STREAM or the accomodations they need, UpSTREAM will exceed student expectations!
